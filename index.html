PRESSURE WASHING VS. SOFT WASHING

The terms are used interchangeably, but pressure washing and soft washing are different tasks both used for professional exterior cleaning. Pressure washing is perfect for most surfaces, but for more delicate jobs, soft washing does the trick.

Soft washing is the ideal approach for glass, siding, and other materials, plus it ensures that they stay cleaner for longer. For all of your service needs in Houston, call us for pressure washing and soft washing, and your satisfaction is guaranteed.
